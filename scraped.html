          Job Details

          Job Description:

          Are you interested in joining a small team with a great culture, where you have the opportunity to wear multiple hats and work on an exciting new project? If so, we invite you to join us!

          Role Overview

          As a Firmware Validation Engineer, you will play a key role in ensuring the quality and reliability of our firmware products. Your responsibilities will include:

          • Test Plan Development and Execution:
            • Participate in the development, execution, and triage of test plans and test cases for Firmware Integration testing.
            • Design, develop, execute, and document test plans and test cases.
          • Coding and Quality Assurance:
            • Practice safe coding principles and respond to Coverity code scans to ensure code quality and security.
          • Pre-Silicon Testing:
            • Support and enable pre-silicon testing in simulation/emulation environments.
          You Should Be Able To Demonstrate

          • Good teamwork and collaboration among coworkers and customers to achieve project goals.
          • Strong communication, documentation, and collaboration skills.

          Join us and be part of a dynamic team where your contributions will make a significant impact!

          Qualifications

          Minimum Qualifications: Candidates must possess the following to be initially considered for this position:

          • Education and Experience:
            • Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Computer Engineering, Electrical Engineering or related field and 3+ years of industry experience related to the degree OR
            • Master's degree in Computer Science, Computer Engineering, Electrical Engineering or related field and 1+ years of industry experience related to the degree,.
            • The years of experience must include:
              • Python programming.
              • Hands-on validation experience.
          Preferred Qualifications: In addition to the minimum requirements, the following qualifications are considered advantageous:

          • Technical Skills:
            • Proficiency in C/C++.
            • Experience with Pytest, Gtest, and FFF.
            • Familiarity with Simics.
          Knowledge of Agile development methodologies.

          Job Type

          Experienced Hire

          Shift

          Shift 1 (United States of America)

          Primary Location:

          US, California, Folsom

          Additional Locations:

          Business Group

          The Client Computing Group (CCG) is responsible for driving business strategy and product development for Intel's PC products and platforms, spanning form factors such as notebooks, desktops, 2 in 1s, all in ones. Working with our partners across the industry, we intend to deliver purposeful computing experiences that unlock people's potential - allowing each person use our products to focus, create and connect in ways that matter most to them. As the largest business unit at Intel, CCG is investing more heavily in the PC, ramping its capabilities even more aggressively, and designing the PC experience even more deliberately, including delivering a predictable cadence of leadership products. As a result, we are able to fuel innovation across Intel, providing an important source of IP and scale, as well as help the company deliver on its purpose of enriching the lives of every person on earth.
